                        🏆 BONUS: PREDICTION CHALLENGE
================================================================================

Before showing ANY results, ask the audience:

"Looking at this product (Whoop/Notion/Canva), which user type do you 
think will have the HARDEST time completing onboarding?"

Show the personas, let them vote, then reveal the data!

Great for:
- Whoop: Most guess "Elite Athletes" will breeze through (correct!)
         but underestimate how badly "Health Beginners" struggle (31%)
         
- Notion: Many assume "Students" are tech-savvy (wrong! 28%)
          The surprise is how much "Team Leads" succeed (71%)
          
- Canva:  "Solo Entrepreneurs" seems easy (68% is decent)
          "Social Creators" struggle more than expected (45%)
